TO THE BEAUTIFUL YOU

FAVOUR GAM

About the Author

Favour Gam is a Cameroonian-born writer, researcher, and storyteller raised in Prince George's County, Maryland. At nineteen, she is a sophomore at the University of Maryland, where she is pursuing a degree in Neuroscience on the pre-medicine track.

Beyond academics, she is passionate about advocacy, particularly in education and health equity, and uses writing as a means to uplift and affirm the voices of Black girls.

As a newly published author with her debut book, "To The Beautiful You: For Colored College Girls" Favour blends personal narrative with cultural reflection, drawing from her experiences as a first-generation African American navigating identity, resilience, and purpose.

About the Book

Pre-order on Amazon Pre-order on Barnes & Noble

Her debut book, To the Beautiful You, is a heartfelt and empowering exploration of the lived experiences of Black girls, navigating the challenges and triumphs of growing up in today's world.

Through a combination of personal anecdotes and thematic essays, the book offers a grounding space for young women to find solace, strength, and the courage to pursue their dreams. The book draws from Favour's own coming-of-age story, blending the personal with the universal to create a narrative that resonates deeply with anyone seeking to understand their worth and purpose.
